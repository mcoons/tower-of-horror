Tower of Horror

This game will challenge your mind.

INSTRUCTIONS

Your goal is to remove groups of matching items until they are all gone. The items must be touching to match.

Click on an item to select it and turn it and it's matching neighbors white. Click on the selected group again to remove it.

Items must be in the front columns (spinning) to click on them.

Remaining items will fall down to fill in the spaces. This happens after levels are rotated also.

Manipulate the tower to remove larger groups which will yield larger scores.

You can rotate each level of the tower with the arrow buttons next to that level.

You can rotate the whole tower with the arrows below the tower.
